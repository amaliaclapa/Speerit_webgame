How to play?

Rules:

  1. A team must have a maximum of 3 players, and there will be a maximum of 3 teams.
  2. Each team takes turns to roll the dice and they pick a card.
  3. The same team who picked a card has a minute to describe the word/concept on the card to the rest of their team without using any word written on the card.
  4. If the team guesses the answer, they advance on the board an equal number of squares with the one shown on the dice.
  5. If the team doesn't get the answer within a minute, the other teams can provide the answer and they get the points and advanced on the board.
  6. When a team lands on a marked square (a coloured square), they pick a yellow card; they have to explain within a minute, but everyone can answer. The point is for the player's team to guess the exact word/concept written on the cards, not synonyms or close meaning words.
  7. If a team couldn't answer the difficult question 3 times, it can choose an easier card, but moves only one space if they guess it within a minute.
  8. The game finishes when one team reaches the finish line .

Don't forget to have fun!
